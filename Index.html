Ducati Multistrada Enduro

Just when you might have thought Ducati had tweaked the Multistrada 1200 as much as you'd think possible, the company took one more stab at it and created an Enduro version. True ADV riders may now have fewer reasons to question the Multistrada's offroad capability. Look no farther than a few of the major changes Ducati made to make the Enduro far more dirt and long-distance friendly.

Shoei X Spirit III helmet

Shoei has always had a reputation for building technologically advanced motorcycle helmets. Its latest flagship model, the X-Spirit III, is no exception with improvements in safety, comfort and aerodynamics over previous flagship models in the company's premium X-series race helmet lineup. Offered earlier this year as the X-Fourteen in North America, the company has taken the same helmet, dubbed it the X-Spirit III, and has now made it available to European racers and riders alike. The X-Spirit III features some unique exterior design twists that the company said make this one of the most aerodynamic helmets available.
